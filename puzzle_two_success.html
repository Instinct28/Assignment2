ESCAPE FROM TECHNOLOGY

A small rock started to move out of the wall, a hidden mechanism revealed a candle. Ryan could see the candle, but he couldn't reach it. And neither could Trent. Ryan looked around the cave again, and he noticed the riddle wasn't there anymore.
"Maybe, we have to press another rock for the candle." Trent said.
"Maybe we don't even need the candle." Ryan said.
"No, we need it. Look there" Trent pointed to the side. There was more cave, except it was much darker than where they were.

Help Ryan find the rock!
